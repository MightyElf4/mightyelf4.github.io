What is This Page?

Well I'm glad you asked! I, like a lot of people, enjoy to eat good food. Not only do I like to eat good food, I like to make it myself. I love the process of learning a recipe, and adapting it to make it my own. Usually, this looks like completely messing up the first time, but also realizing what I need to change in order to do better next time. I enjoy researching how to make a meal, gathering and preparing the ingredients, then focusing on actually cooking the meal, and finally sharing it with my family or friends.

With that said, the purpose of this page is to act as a depository for all the things I have cooked or want to cook in the future. Like all things on this website, I am currently developing it, and most likely it will go through a lot of changes before I finally reach a form that is to my liking. As of right now, I am picturing adding links and images to web pages that I used to research the dishes I like to make, or want to make, adding images, and eventually uploading my own recipes.
